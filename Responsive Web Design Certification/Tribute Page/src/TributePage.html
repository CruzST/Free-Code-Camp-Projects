Makoto Shinkai (新海 誠)

Anime Film Director

Shinkai during an inerview on the production of "Your Name"

Makoto Shinki is a prominent anime film director in the industry. In recent years he was produced film hits such as "Graden of Words" and "Your Name". Influenced by titans such as Hayao Miyazaki, Makoto Shinki continutes to pave way to make his mark in anime history.

Shinkai began his career as a video game designer for the company Falcom. In 1999 he release a monochrome film, "She and Her Cat" for the DoGA CG Animation contest in 2000, a contest for entry-level animators. Upon winning the contest Shinkai would remain at Falcom but continue to create animations. In 2001 he quit his job at Falcom to pursue the production of "Voices of a Distant Star" full time which would be released in 2002. Shinkai's film releases would continue to be praised one after another by critics and fans alike. His most recent release "Your Name" currently stands as the highest-grossing anime film of all time. A title previously held by "Spirited Away" directed by the reknown Hayao Miyazaki. Shinkai's next filim "Weathering With You" is anticipated for release in 2019.
