Bang Dream!

BanG Dream! (バンドリ!, Bandori!) is a Japanese multimedia project developed by Bushiroad. BanG Dream! has released music CD's, anime music videos, live concert videos, manga adaptions, games, card games, and an anime series.

Bushiroad launched the project in January 2015. Poppin'Party was formed in February 2015. With the creation of the mobile game, four other bands were created namely Afterglow, Pastel*Palettes, Roselia, and Hello, Happy World!

An all-girl band project, THE THIRD, was announced in January of 2018 later renamed to RAISE A SUILEN. RAISE A SUILEN is not a part of the mobile game however they do have a role in in season 2 of the show.

Currently the the mobile application game, "BanG Dream! Girls Band Party!" ranked No. 38 in Japan for Top Grossing in 2018.



Poppin' Party

A girl band always searching for that sparkling, heart-pounding beat, based on Toyama Kasumi's (guitar and vocals) childhood observation of the stars shining in the night sky. The other four members are music-lover Hanazono Tae (lead guitar), shy but confident girl Ushigome Rimi (bass), kind and family-oriented Yamabuki Saaya (drums), and constant contrarian Ichigaya Arisa (keyboard). They have band practice in Arisa's basement, and what they lack in skill and experience, they more than make up for in enthusiasm.


After Glow

The group consists of five members, namely Mitake Ran (guitar and vocals), Aoba Moca (guitar), Uehara Himari (bass), Udagawa Tomoe (drums), and Hazawa Tsugumi (keyboard). A girl band made up of childhood friends. When Ran moved to a different class, the girls made a band so they could still see each other. Now in high school, they love playing in their band, practicing after school and playing live shows. They get along famously and almost never fight. Though they're still fairly inexperienced, they gained popularity for their powerful performances and strong vocals.


Pastel Palettes

An idol band formed by a talent agency. The band is made up of Maruyama Aya (vocals), who has spent huge amounts of time training for her debut; celebrity and child actor Shirasagi Chisato (bass), and modeling sensation Wakamiya Eve (keyboard). They were later joined by Hikawa Hina (guitar), who passed the band's auditions and then studio musician Yamato Maya (drums). The five of them are using this experience to bolster their very own definition of an "idol" and what is it to be one.


Roselia

A highly talented band. With their skilled performances and pro-level vocals, they are a highly praised group. Minato Yukina (vocals) started the band with a special certain goal in mind. The other members are the like-minded Hikawa Sayo (guitar), childhood friend Imai Lisa (bass), youngest and most talented Udagawa Ako (drums), and the award-winning Shirokane Rinko (keyboard). They play at a well-known live house and hope someday to make it to the big time.


Hello, Happy World!

A band formed on the concept of "Making the world smile." The super optimist and crazy rich Tsurumaki Kokoro (vocals), the beautiful and ever-popular Setou Kaoru (guitar), sporty Kitazawa Hagumi (bass), the kind-hearted and girly Matsubara Kanon (drums), and calm, cool and collected Okusawa Misaki (DJ) make up this band. They often perform at pre-schools and children's hospitals, making them popular with the youngsters.


Other Bands



RAISE A SUILEN


Since the voice actresses of Afterglow, Pastel*Palettes or Hello, Happy World! are unable to play their characters' instrument, RAISE A SUILEN was created to allow the vocalists to have a chance to perform. However, the band also has their own original songs. The band members play roles of characters within the Bang Dream anime.

Glitter Green


The group consists of four members, namely Ushigome Yuri on guitar and vocals, Uzawa Rii on bass, Wanibe Nanana on keyboard and Nijikki Hinako on drums. The band played a minor role within the anime however their songs do appear in the mobile game.
